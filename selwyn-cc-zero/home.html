About me

My name is Selwyn Versteeg.
Web designer and web developer with a focus on the user.
Coding is what I do best but I also love designing and prototyping. Quality is important, spending time on details and optimizing solutions is what I can't resist to do. I'm a perfectionist.

Currently I'm studying Communication and Multimedia Design at the HvA. At CMD I learn to work agile in a team and come up with creative solutions.
When I'm not busy with my study, coding or crafting a design. I like to jam a bit on my funky drumset, ride my bike or play video games.
