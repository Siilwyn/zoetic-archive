What's the purpose?

To let people think creatively about their future. Now is the time for a new ideas with a new systems. People are starting to realize that they do not want to be dominated by the government anymore. This opens a new approach. Through social media you always stay in touch with the world. And because it is an online community you can do business with everyone, anytime anywere. So, you have an idea?

Developing an idea into a successful private company is an adventure. A lot of unexpected and unfamiliar problems can come up. Much misery can be avoided if you work out the idea in an early stage to some kind of perfection. You will then find out if the idea really is so brilliant.

Do you want to check your idea?

First, it is wise to check the market with regard to innovation and feasibility. Second, check the idea with friends, acquaintances, specialists and potential customers. The wider you submit your plans to different audiences, the clearer the opportunities and challenges reveal themselves.
